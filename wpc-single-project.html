Wedding Pro Connect
Mobile App

The Wedding Pro Connect App is designed to help engaged couples easily create the wedding of their dreams within their budget. It lets them view a multitude of vendors add them to a list of vendors and it totals that list to give them an accurate estimate of what their dream wedding may actually cost. They can then share these wedding profiles on social media.
